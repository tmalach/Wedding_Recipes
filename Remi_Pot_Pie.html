Instructions

  1. Assemble Pie:
    • In a bowl, mix flour, pumpkin purée, and olive oil.
    • Gradually add chicken broth until a dough forms.
    • Roll out dough on a floured surface. Cut the dough into multiple sheets to fit ramekins. Make sure the dough is sufficiently thin to line the ramekins.
    • Set aside extra dough if you’d like a top crust or decorative shapes.
    • Chill crust in fridge while preparing filling.
  2. Preheat Oven:
    • Preheat to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Cook Filling:
    • In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped chicken and cook for about 3 minutes.
    • Add carrots and green beans/peas and cook for another 5 minutes.
    • Add flour and cook for 1–3 minutes.
    • Pour in broth slowly and mix to avoid clumping.
    • Add pumpkin purée, and simmer 5–10 minutes. If needed, dissolve 1 tablespoon of cornstarch in 1 tablespoon of water, mix, and add to thicken.
    • Allow the mixture to cool before assembling the pies.
  4. Bake:
    • Line the bottom and sides of each ramekin with chilled dough.
    • Fill with cooled chicken mixture.
    • Top with crust (optional). Crimp the edges with a fork.
    • Bake for 20–35 minutes until golden brown. Cool fully before serving.

Notes

  • Dog-friendly IngredientsMake sure to use dog friendly ingredients. Avoid members of the allium family (onion, garlic, shallots, leeks, etc.). Avoid overly salty broths and other spices. Check online to make sure any added ingredients are safe. Ensure no ingredients contain fillers like xylitol which are extremely toxic to dogs.
  • Calories: Each pie should contain 18 g protein, 12 g fat, 32 g carbohydrates, and 4 g fiber for a total of 310 calories (according to AI estimate).
